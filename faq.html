Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers before you reserve your seat

What is the dress code?

Dress Code: Sapologie. Expect bold tailoring, refined silhouettes, statement accessories, and confident color. This is a celebration of style as identity — every detail speaks. For inspiration, explore our Instagram and TikTok.

What's included in my ticket?

Entry includes access to the red carpet, welcome cocktails, gala dinner, keynote presentations, curated fashion moments, entertainment, and networking opportunities.

Can I get a refund?

All ticket sales are final. However, tickets are transferable up to 48 hours before the event.

Is parking available?

Yes. Emara Ole-Sereni offers secure parking. VIP ticket holders receive complimentary valet parking.

Are photos and videos allowed?

Absolutely. Professional photographers will be present, and guests are welcome to capture and share their experience.

Will there be networking opportunities?

Yes. Networking is a central part of the gala, with dedicated moments throughout the evening.

Is the venue wheelchair accessible?

Yes. Emara Ole-Sereni has ramps, elevators, and accessible restrooms.

Can I bring a guest?

Each ticket admits one person. For guests, please purchase additional tickets.

Still Have Questions?

Reach us directly and we will help you prepare for the gala.
